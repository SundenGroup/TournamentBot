What it does

🎮

15 games + custom

CS2, VALORANT, League, Rocket League, GOALS and more — each with tuned defaults. Or run any game you like.

🏆

4 formats

Single & double elimination, Swiss, and round robin — byes, seeding and walkovers handled automatically.

💬

Private match rooms

Every match gets its own channel for the two sides plus admins. Coordinate, play, report.

🌐

Live web brackets

A shareable, auto-updating bracket page for every tournament — right here on tournaments.clutch.game.
